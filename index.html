About us

We are ‘Flare Creative,’ a recently started company specialized in graphic design. Flare Creative consists of two members, Melvin de Witte (eightteen years old) and Bas van de Groep (seventeen years old) based in Arnhem, The Netherlands. The reason why we started this company is because of we both share a passion and thought it would be great to combine them.

Melvin de Witte

I am Melvin, an eightteen years old graphic designer who lives in Elst, a place next to Arnhem in the Netherlands. My main skill is logo, 2D and advertisments design. I also like to photography and film, I mainly do this for school projects or for myself. Of course we are trying to improve ourselfs, I am busy trying my best with photo manipulation.

HOW I STARTED WITH DESIGNING. It all began as a hobby, I started designing YouTube banners for a friend of mine who had a channel and need a banner, avatar and some thumbnails. I started improving myself in designing banners for Youtubers and became populair and I was getting orders, from that moment I started to design for money.

WHAT ARE MY FUTURE PLANS? I am currently in school and follow the education Media design. At the moment I am in first grade of my study and I have three more years to go. During school I would like to invest much time as I can in our starting company, so when I am graduated I could spend my knowledge and time in design and enterprise.
